Italian Caprese Salad

Description
The finished product: A classic Italian salad, made of sliced fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and sweet basil, seasoned with salt, and olive oil. Perfect for a light lunch or a summer starter!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into 1/4 inch thick slices.
  2. Arrange alternating slices of tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves on a large serving platter.
  3. Drizzle the salad with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. If desired, finish the dish with a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
